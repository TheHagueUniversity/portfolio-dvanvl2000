Design Competition: Sustainable City of the Future

I worked on a project during high school that had us design a medium sized sustainable city, based on our own (Gouda). In a group of 7 students we worked on making a sustainable city on paper, and in the end had to present and compete against other groups. This project already gave me some insight about designing for a particular situation (a city without fossil fuels and unsafe of floods) and user-group (the people of the city).

UXD Project Bespoke Design

During the first year of my current study we were given a project to work on by Hallmark that let us get acquainted with the designing process of UX. At the same time it taught us the ways and rules of designing for a user and user group. In this project we had to come up with a card personalisation tool and an unwrapping experience, which was based on research data of a user whose data was then merged with another to create a persona.
